Frequently asked questions

Which ones are silver and which golden supporters?

The golden supporters are the more prominent ones with an image, placed on the red page in the image above. The silver supporters are on the opposite (white) page and presented with name alone.

When will I receive the book?

The books will go to print once all or most of the 20 supporter positions are sold. If not enough editions are sold it will not go to print at all (and the purchases will be refunded).

Can a company be a supporter?

Yes, you can support the project as a company. In the case when a company is a golden supporter its logo will be used instead of the photo. The company website can be used in the second line of the entry.

Will there be more of these available later?

For now, only the unique and customized 20 are available. If the project is successful and demand for this version high, I will consider making more of the hardcover editions available later.
