About

Web design is 95% typography. But still, typography is one of the most neglected subjects in web design. Poor choice of typefaces and low screen resolutions have been somewhat forcing web designers to do so. Consequently, there's millions of Internet users worldwide that are stuck with poor readability and accessibility of websites. Even after the @font-face revolution a few years ago, the web is still barely readable. More options and more combinations of poor typefaces won't solve the problem.

Taking the rules from graphic design & typography and applying them to the web is the only way to really improve web typography. It's Betterwebtype.com's goal to do that by properly educating web designers and developers through lessons and examples.
